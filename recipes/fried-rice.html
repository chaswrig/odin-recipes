Vegetable Fried Rice

Simple fried rice that can be stretched with additional vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked rice
  • 3 TBS soy sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1 bag diced frozen vegetables

Directions

  1. Cook rice in rice cooker
  2. Add rice and frozen vegetables in skillet
  3. Heat, season to taste, and serve
