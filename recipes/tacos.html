Tacos

Ingredients

  • Taco shells
  • Ground beef
  • Shredded cheese
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatos
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Recipe Steps

  1. Brown ground beef, drain
  2. Heat taco shells
  3. Add meat to shells
  4. Add cheese on top of meat
  5. Add lettuce on top of shells
  6. Top with tomatos, sour cream, and salsa
