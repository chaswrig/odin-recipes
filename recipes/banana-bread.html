Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • Five large bananas
  • Two cups flour
  • Half cup sugar
  • Half cup brown sugar
  • Half cup vegetable or olive oil
  • 1 TSP baking soda
  • 1 TSP baking powder
  • 1 TSP vanilla extract
  • 1/2 TSP salt

Cooking Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients in bowl
  2. Pour into greased bread pan
  3. Bake for 70 minutes
  4. Let rest for 10 minutes in pan, then one hour on cooling rack
  5. Slice and serve
