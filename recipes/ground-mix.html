Ground Meat, Corn, and Beans

Simple three-ingredient hot meal. Can be seasoned to taste for variety and served with rice or bread for additional calories

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of any ground meat (beef, turkey, chicken, pork)
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 can pinto beans

Directions

  1. Cook ground meat to temperature
  2. Add corn and beans to ground meat
  3. Heat, season to taste, and serve
